The Problem

Deepfakes are winning.
Metadata isn't enough.

Content credentials can be stripped with a single re-encode. AI detection is losing ground — deepfake volume is growing far faster than detection accuracy. The world needs provenance that's bound to the content itself, not stapled on as metadata.

Our approach

We've developed a proprietary verification protocol that embeds provenance directly into media content. It survives compression, re-encoding, and platform re-uploads. When someone tampers with the content, the proof breaks — and we can pinpoint exactly where.

The verification results are anchored on-chain via Solana, creating an immutable, public record. $VORTEX is the token that powers this verification network.

Patent pending. Core algorithm details available under NDA to qualified partners.
